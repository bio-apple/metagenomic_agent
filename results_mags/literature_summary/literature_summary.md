# Literature Summary

## Bacteroides
- Interpretation: Polysaccharide utilization and immunomodulatory metabolite production.
- Paper: Bacteroides ecology (kb:bact-1) 

## Faecalibacterium
- Interpretation: Produces butyrate that fuels colonocytes and supports epithelial barrier integrity.
- Paper: Faecalibacterium prausnitzii and human health (kb:faecali-1) 

## Prevotella
- Interpretation: Fiber-associated community member influencing mucosal immune tone.
- Paper: Prevotella and diet (kb:prev-1) 

## Bifidobacterium
- Interpretation: SCFA production and early-life colonization benefits.
- Paper: Bifidobacterium and gut health (kb:bifi-1) 

## Roseburia
- Interpretation: Butyrate-producing Firmicute supporting gut barrier function.
- Paper: Roseburia butyrate producers (kb:rose-1)
